Install on Your Phone

Android (Chrome)

  1. 1 Open this website in Chrome browser
  2. 2 Tap the three dots menu (⋮) in the top right corner
  3. 3 Tap "Add to Home screen" or "Install app"
  4. 4 Tap "Add" to confirm - the app icon will appear on your home screen!

iPhone / iPad (Safari)

  1. 1 Open this website in Safari browser (not Chrome)
  2. 2 Tap the Share button (square with arrow pointing up) at the bottom of the screen
  3. 3 Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  4. 4 Tap "Add" in the top right - the app icon will appear on your home screen!

Note: Once installed, the app works offline and opens like a native app without the browser address bar. Your data syncs across devices when signed in.
