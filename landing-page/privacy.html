Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 5, 2026

Introduction

Seasons Past ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our MTG Commander Tracker application.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: If you choose to sign in with Google, we receive your email address and display name from Google to create your account.
  • Game Data: Information you enter about your decks, game history, wins, losses, opponents, and statistics.
  • Usage Data: Basic analytics about how you use the app to help us improve the experience.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide and maintain the app's functionality
  • Sync your data across devices when you're signed in
  • Display your game statistics and history
  • Improve the app based on usage patterns

Data Storage

Local Storage: If you use the app without signing in, all your data is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data.

Cloud Storage: If you sign in with Google, your data is stored in Firebase (Google Cloud) to enable syncing across devices. This data is associated with your account and is not shared with third parties.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. Your game data is yours and remains private to your account.

We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • With your consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights or the safety of users

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication: For secure sign-in with Google
  • Firebase Firestore: For cloud data storage and sync
  • Scryfall API: For fetching Magic: The Gathering card images and data (no personal data is shared)

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. Data transmitted to our servers is encrypted using HTTPS. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data associated with your account within the app
  • Export: Download your data in JSON or CSV format
  • Delete: Remove your account and all associated data
  • Use Offline: Use the app without an account, keeping all data local

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
