Powerful Features for all levels of EDH Players

Seasons Past has everything you need to track and analyze each game of Commander you play

Deck Management

Construct an unlimited Commander library with all of your favourite decks. Organize and tweak your decks with Moxfield and EDHREC built directly into the app.

Game Logging

Record wins, losses, and opponents. The app tracks all your games and keeps a detailed history of your Commander matches.

Statistics

Win rates, streaks, color breakdowns, and more. Visualize your performance with real-time analytics.

Life Counter

EDH life-counter feature built right into the app for up to 6 players. Track life totals, commander damage, and poison counters without having to swtich apps.

Cloud Sync

Seasons Past Web App syncs your data across devices using Google Cloud. Your data is always backed up and accessible wherever you play.

POD Buddies

Seasons Past Web App has a built in POD Buddies system. Add your friends and track your games together. See how you stack up!
