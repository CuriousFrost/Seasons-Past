The Core

Outcome-focused features

  • Deck Building: Store your decklists in one searchable index.
  • Stats Engine: Track win rates, streaks, and color-identity patterns.
  • Pod Buddies: Stack rivalries and review past matchups.
  • Life Counter: Run gameplay tools without ever leaving the app.
  • Cloud-Based: Save your data in the cloud to ensure your logs are available everywhere.
  • Advanced Filters: See data or stats from any year, commander, or player-matchup.
